Cookie Recipe

My great-grandmother's christmas cookie recipe. The dough is incredibly simple to make using few and inexpensive ingrediants.

But the difficult part comes in preparing it. As you roll, you need to make sure the dough is remaining more-or-less solid.

If it gets too warm it will stick to the rolling pin, your hands, and the rolling surface. Good luck!

  • 1lb 10x(powdered) sugar
  • 3/4 lbs butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 c flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Throughougly beat butter and sugar together
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, beat
  4. Add flour
  5. Chill overnight
  6. Cut dough into quaters
  7. Working with one quater at a time, roll thin and use cookie cutters to cut out shapes
  8. Place on greased baking sheet
  9. Add sprinkles (optional, but very desirable
  10. Bake for 10 minutes; YOU DO NOT WANT THEM GETTING BROWNED
